Privacy Policy for Cuty - Live Video Chat

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

Welcome to Cuty - Live Video Chat ("App", "we", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera & Microphone Data: Real-time video and audio streams are processed locally on your device and transmitted directly peer-to-peer (via WebRTC encryption) to establish live video calls. We do not store or record your video calls on our servers.
  • Device & Usage Information: Device model, OS version, app performance logs, and unique session identifiers for app functionality.
  • User-Generated Content & Reports: Text messages sent during live chats, block/report logs for user safety and moderation.

2. User Safety & Content Moderation

Cuty strictly prohibits harassment, explicit content, hate speech, or illegal behavior. We provide an in-app Report & Block button on all video call interfaces. Users reported for violations will be suspended or permanently banned.

3. How We Use Information

  • To facilitate real-time peer-to-peer random video matching and direct video calls.
  • To process coin balances, VIP features, and in-app interactions.
  • To ensure app security, prevent fraud, and enforce Google Play policy guidelines.

4. Data Security

All video and audio communications use standard WebRTC encryption protocols (DTLS/SRTP). Your streams are transmitted securely directly between call participants.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact support at: support@cutylive.app
